Richard Avedon

Richard Avedon (1923-2004) One of the greatest photographers of the 20th century.

Famous for his excellent portraits and bold, direct styles.

He has worked for Harper's bazaar and the New Yorker, and has taken pictures of several famous magazines. He is a recognized master of photography in the industry.

Richard Avedon has been so dedicated and focused on his work, that he has won several prestigious photography awards and the honorary titles of several prestigious universities.

He spent all his life on the road of photography, and finally died of a sudden cerebral hemorrhage at work.

Richard avedon has had many photography exhibitions, and his works has been preserved by different art museums, to honor the talented and talented photographer.
